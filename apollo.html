Apollo

Autonomous robot for campus interaction and navigation

Overview

Apollo is our flagship autonomous robot project designed to interact with people on campus while navigating complex environments. The robot combines computer vision, natural language processing, and advanced path planning algorithms to create meaningful interactions with students, faculty, and visitors.

The project focuses on developing robust human-robot interaction capabilities, including voice recognition, gesture understanding, and social navigation behaviors. Apollo serves as a platform for testing cutting-edge robotics research in real-world campus environments, from providing directions to engaging in educational conversations about AI and robotics.

Requirements

Familiarity with Python or C++
Interest in robotics and AI
Familarity with Git for version control
