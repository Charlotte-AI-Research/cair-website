Neural Encoding

Decoding neural signals using EEG and deep learning

Overview

Project Lead: Brian Mohseni

The Neural Encoding project focuses on understanding and decoding brain activity patterns using electroencephalography (EEG) combined with advanced deep learning techniques. Our goal is to develop systems that can interpret neural signals in real-time, opening possibilities for brain-computer interfaces and neurological research applications.

We use state-of-the-art machine learning models to process EEG data, identifying patterns that correspond to different cognitive states, motor intentions, and neural responses. This research has potential applications in assistive technology, neurofeedback systems, and advancing our understanding of brain function.

Requirements

Familiarity with Python
Interest in neuroscience and brain-computer interfaces
Basic understanding of machine learning
