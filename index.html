About Us

Charlotte AI Research is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing the field of artificial intelligence through collaborative research, education, and innovation. We bring together passionate students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the frontiers of AI technology and its applications.

Our mission is to foster a community of learners and innovators who are committed to developing ethical, responsible AI solutions that benefit society. Through workshops, seminars, research projects, and industry partnerships, we provide our members with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the AI field.
